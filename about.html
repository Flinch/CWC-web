Welcome to ChildCare & Wellness Clinics


Where you will receive outstanding care provided by top-ranked physicians and nurses. ChildCare & Wellness Clinics is a medical center located at 26 Anthony Enahoro street Utako District of the Federal Capital Territory, in Nigeria.


CWC is a family clinic which provides excellent healthcare service delivery to children as well as their moms and dads. In addition to being a health care service delivery the facility is also a public health research center which carries out public health research with various institutes both locally and internationally.


Our centers of excellence include Pediatrics Specialty and Well- check Clinics, Physiotherapy and Ultrasonography, Orthopedic Specialty, Neurology, ENT Specialty, General and Specialist surgeries including Paediatrics Surgeries and more.


We at CWC believe in four core values and you will see it evident the moment you step into the clinic. These core values are;

Honesty

Honesty in the way we conduct our work, transparency in communication, and looking out for our patients.

Respect

We beleive in respect for our patients, our staff and every single person that walks through the doors of our clinic.

Comfort

We will alway prioritze your comfort. If there is anything we can do to elevate this, we will.

Safety & Cleanliness

We have taken steps to ensure that your safety, and the cleanliness of our environment are levels beyond what is standard.

Dr Yashua

Dr Yashua Alkali Hamza, is a Pediatrician and Public health Expert with vast experience in the area of project implementation, evaluation and research. She works with various agencies, national and international NGO's and Public health institutions around the world on community health and health promotion activities. Some of her current partners include the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine (LSHTM), the Institute of International Education (IIE) based in New York, and the Common Heritage Foundation based in Nigeria.

She has also worked with other organizations like the Harvard School of Public health and the New York University Research Centre for Leadership in Action (NYU RCLA) on health promotion and research activities. She has pioneered, led, and supervised various community-based projects focused on improving the lives and health of disadvantaged women and children, using innovative and groundbreaking techniques. She co-founded Family Included , is a Member of the Nigerian Medical Association (NMA), American Public Health Association (APHA) and the American Medical Writers Association (AMWA).

She is a fellow of the West African College of Physicians (FWACP) and the Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC). She has a Masters of Public Health degree from the University of Liverpool, and is currently a Doctoral candidate at Walden University. She is also the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Childcare and Wellness Clinics, located in Abuja, Nigeria. And yes, I love books, lavender and quirky chairs.

Monisola Ogunsanya

The Medical Director of Childcare and Wellness Clinics is a Consultant Family Physician who specialises in providing medical care to both the young and old within the family and community. Dr. Monisola Ogunsanya, with her understanding that illness and disease are modified by family dynamics and one’s environment, has provided medical care to clients of various ages, ethnic backgrounds in both urban and rural communities for over a decade.

She was the head of a team which analysed the quality of healthcare given to clients in a secondary health facility and has been actively involved with several teams in establishing measures and protocols to improve quality of care. She subsequently became the site coordinator for the facility offering care to both urban and rural communities over a period of three years. Dr. Monisola has worked as a facilitator/trainer of healthcare workers of various cadres in assessing and improving quality of care in their health facilities. She is a fellow of the National Postgraduate Medical College of Nigeria and a Member of The Society of Family Physicians of Nigeria (SOFPON).
