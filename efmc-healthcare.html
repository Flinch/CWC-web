EFMC HEALTHCARE AND RESEARCH PROJECT

December 11, 2023

Partnering for a Healthier Future

Childcare and Wellness is proud to join forces with EFMC in the fight against HIV. Together, we're committed to reducing the prevalence of HIV in our communities and providing comprehensive care for those living with the disease.

Our HIV Clinic Services:

  • Confidential HIV Counseling and Testing: Receive personalized support and accurate testing in a safe, judgment-free environment.
  • Seamless Referral to EFMC Treatment: We ensure prompt access to expert care and treatment options for those who test positive.
  • Free HIV Testing: Take charge of your health with our complimentary testing services, accessible to everyone.
  • Convenient Medication Pickup and Sample Referral: Streamlining care and support for clients enrolled in treatment programs.

Advancing Research and Care

  • HIV Adherence Research: Investigating factors that influence adherence to treatment, with the goal of developing more effective support strategies.
  • Behavioral Change Interventions: Exploring innovative approaches to promote healthy behaviors and reduce HIV transmission.

Your Health is Our Priority

By joining this global network, Nigeria will both learn from and contribute to international best practices. The research component will ensure that strategies are tailored to the local context, maximizing the impact of interventions and ultimately improving the health and well-being of mothers, fathers, and children across Nigeria.
