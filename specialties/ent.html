Ear, Nose & Throat


We need our ears, our noses and our throats for so many of our daily functions. Just one malfunction can throw the whole body’s function out of kilter. The senses of smell, taste and hearing as well as our balance are dependent on these three structures. Any defect in these areas can also affect a child’s growth, development and ability to learn.


This is why the care of these aspects of your body is important to us in Childcare and Wellness Clinics. Our Ear, Nose and Throat (ENT) Specialist, also known as the Otolarygologist, and his team are here to educate you, prevent, screen for and solve all the breathing, smelling, tasting, hearing and balance problems you and your family may experience. There is care for the old and the young, male or female.
