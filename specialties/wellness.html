For Wellness


Feeling under the weather? Something not quite right? Maybe you just want to check, catch stuff early. Our Wellness checks require no appointments or registrations. Open 24/7 all you have to do is show up, we have got you covered. Discover all the wellness checks we have to offer below

School Entry Health Check


Our school entry health check is available to students of all ages.

Well Baby Checks & Complete Adult Health Checks


Whether you're a full sized human being or a tiny one. We have checks designed to go over every inch of your health.

Pre-Employment Screening


Need to get that employement screening settled for that shiny new job? We've got you covered!

Travel Checks and Vaccination


Going to Malibu? Need to get vaccinated? Sometimes those two go hand in hand. We'll take care of that for you.
