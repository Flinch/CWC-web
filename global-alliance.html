December 11, 2023

Nigeria Joins Global Effort to Promote Father Involvement in Maternal and Child Health, Enhancing Care Through Research

Childcare and Wellness Clinics is proud to be the principal partner of the Family Included Global Alliance in Nigeria, advocating for greater inclusion of fathers in maternal and child health. This initiative recognizes the crucial role fathers play in the well-being of mothers and children, aligning with WHO's declaration of family-inclusive maternal healthcare as a top global priority.

Backed by WHO research, Family Included aims to realize the vision of family-inclusive maternal healthcare through a multi-faceted approach:

  • Professional Training: Equipping healthcare workers with skills to confidently engage men, couples, and groups in care, ensuring comprehensive support for the entire family unit.
  • Information Dissemination: Educating fathers on their crucial role and providing resources for families, empowering them to actively participate in the care journey.
  • Networking Opportunities: Building support systems for healthcare workers and fostering collaboration among policymakers, creating a united front for family-centered care.
  • Research Initiatives: Conducting research to understand the specific needs and challenges faced by fathers in the Nigerian context, informing evidence-based interventions and policies.

By joining this global network, Nigeria will both learn from and contribute to international best practices. The research component will ensure that strategies are tailored to the local context, maximizing the impact of interventions and ultimately improving the health and well-being of mothers, fathers, and children across Nigeria.
