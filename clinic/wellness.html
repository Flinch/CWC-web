For Wellness


Childcare And Wellness Clinics Wellness Package Offer Adults Only Primary Health Care In The Clinic. It Comprises Of Either Of The Following:

Complete adult health check package:

Patients can only book for an appointment or visit the Clinic to see a doctor at all times. The front desk personnel will require the patient’s data for registration as a new patient. Only after clearing with the front desk personnel will the patient be ushered in to see the doctor.


During consultation, the patient will be asked some questions in order to make accurate diagnosis. The doctor will inform you of any necessary laboratory test to be carried out, should the need arise. During consultation a patient will be informed if he/she will be admitted into the Clinic or treated as an outpatient. Cervical cancer screening and vaccination is also offered to patients on demand and charged.


At the Laboratory a specialist will collect samples necessary for the test, bill patient for it and informs the patient of the time to collect the results. Laboratory Results are printed and given to patients. The doctor makes a diagnosis based on the laboratory results gotten where necessary and sends you to the pharmacy. At the pharmacy drugs are given to the patient and billed.


All payments are made at the account section in order to carry out required services. Subsequent visits do not require registration except another patient is using same folder

Ultrasonographys


Patients are booked (link) to have ultrasonography in the clinic for an appointment. The front desk personnel will require the patient’s data for registration as a new patient. Only after clearing with the front desk personnel will the patient be ushered in to see the doctor.


After the ultrasound scan the results are explained and given to the patient with advice. All payments are made at the account section of the Clinic and receipts issued consequently.


Domestic Staff Health Check


Childcare and Wellness Clinic offers complete domestic staff health check to clients on demand. It does not require appointments and can be done 24hours a day. The front desk will require patient’s data for registration, which will be billed for and only after clearing with the front desk personnel will the patient be ushered in to see a doctor.


The doctor refers the patient to the Laboratory Department of Clinic which collects samples and informs the patient when the results will be ready. The doctor explains and gives the result to the patient, as well as advice accordingly. Patient will be placed on treatment should it be required.


All payments are made at the account section of the Clinic and receipts issued consequently.


Pre-Employment Check


The Pre-employment check package of Childcare and Wellness Clinic offers organizations health check for prospective staff on demand. It does not require appointments and can be done 24hours a day. The front desk will require patient’s data for registration, which will be billed for and only after clearing with the front desk personnel will the patient be ushered in to see a doctor.


The doctor refers the patient to the Laboratory Department of Clinic which collects samples and informs the patient when the results will be ready. The doctor explains and gives the result to the patient, as well as advice accordingly. Patient will be placed on treatment should it be required.


All payments are made at the account section of the Clinic and receipts issued consequently.


Well Woman


The Well-woman specialty of the Clinic offers women of reproductive ages obstetrics and gynecology advice and treatment to patients only. Patient are required to book for this service of the Clinic. The Clinic does not offer Antenatal Package or care however provides advice and referrals to patients that require it. Gynaecological cases are handled by the Obstetrician/Gynaecologist of Childcare and Wellness Clinics.


At the Clinic the front desk personnel will require patient’s data for registration, which will be billed for, and only after clearing with the front desk personnel will the patient be ushered in to see a doctor. The doctor refers patient to the Laboratory Department of Clinic, which collects samples and informs the patient when the results will be ready.


The doctor explains and gives the result to the patient, as well as advice accordingly. Patient will be placed on treatment should it be required. All payments are made at the account section of the Clinic and receipts issued consequently.
